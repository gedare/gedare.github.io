A bit more about me
I was born and raised outside of Eagle River, WI, a small resort town in the Northwoods of Wisconsin. I graduated high school in Brentwood, TN, college at Michigan Tech in Houghton, MI ( Da U.P.), and graduate school at GWU in our nation's capital. I got my Ph.D. in order to become a professor like my role model, my grandfather, the late Laird W. Heal ([Google scholar]).

I have 3 brothers, 1 sister, 2 cats, a wonderful wife and 3 sweet children. Outside of work, I enjoy cycling, jogging, strength training, coding for fun, saunas, coffee, long walks in the woods, swimming in lakes, meeting new people, and spending time with my family.
